  • About Me

    GIS Enthusiast. Software Developer. Cat Dad.

    Born and raised in Michigan, I earned a bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a concentration in Geographic Information Systems from the Univeristy of Michigan - Flint. After a brief period as a doctoral student and teaching assistant in Applied Mathematics at Oakland University, I left purely academic pursuits and began my career as a software developer. I am now also a graduate student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, in the Online GIS Development program, with the express purpose of sharpening my GIS skills. Through creative and technical analysis, I hope to put these and my other accrued skills to use solving spatial problems. My full resume is available here: resume


    In my spare time, I am an avid reader, Dungeons & Dragons player, track and field fan, and try to get out and run as often as possible. I've had the distinct pleasure of attending several major events at Hayward field, and take any opportunity I can to visit the running mecca.


    I also spend a decent amount of time an eye on my cats, making sure they don't get up to too much trouble.
