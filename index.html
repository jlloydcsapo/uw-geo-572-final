  • About Me

    GIS Enthusiast. Software Developer. Cat Dad.

    Born and raised in Michigan, I recieved my bachelor's degree from the Univeristy of Michigan - Flint in Mathematics. I also had a concentration in Geographic Information Systems. This pairing has led me to the firm belief that creative analysis is the most effective way to gain insight

    In my spare time, I am an avid reader, Dungeons & Dragons player, and track and field fan, and try to get and run as often as possible. I've had the distinct pleasure of attending several major events at Hayward field, and take any opportunity I can to visit the running mecca.

    I also spend a decent amount of time an eye on my cats, and making sure they don't get up to too much trouble.
